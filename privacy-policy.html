Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Road Shooter handles data when you use this app.

1. Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores gameplay progress (for example stage, upgrades, and settings) in your browser local storage so your progress can continue between sessions.

2. Analytics (Optional)

Analytics is disabled by default in this launch package. If enabled by the publisher through js/shared/launch-config.js, analytics services may collect usage information such as page views and session events.

3. Advertising (Optional)

Ads are disabled by default in this launch package. If enabled by the publisher through js/shared/launch-config.js, third-party ad providers may use cookies or similar technologies according to their own policies.

4. Third-Party Services

If analytics or ads are enabled, data handling is also subject to the applicable third-party policy. Example providers may include Google Analytics and Google AdSense.

5. Children

This app is a general audience game and does not intentionally collect personal information from children.

6. Contact

For privacy questions, contact support via GitHub Issues.
